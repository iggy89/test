A Little bit about Jamesy

He was born on September 26th, 1988 as James Blake Litherland in England. He was classically trained on piano and graduated with a degree in popular music.

He first gained attention for his cover of Feists 'Limit to Your Love' in 2011. In January 2019 he released his fourth album titled Assume Form.
